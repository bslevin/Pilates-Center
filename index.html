Pilates is a full body workout that tones, strengthens, and lengthens muscles, while continuously working the core

- without building bulk!

Our Classes

Reformer Classes

Pilates exercises using spring-loaded machines to guide the body during the exercise into the correct form.

Mat Classes

Mat Pilates is a strengthening and lengthening form of exercise that focuses on your core (trunk) muscles while also training your arms and legs.

Prenatal Classes

Prenatal pilates classes are specifically designed to strengthen and improve flexibility, strength and recovery after giving birth.

Couple Classes

Couples, friends, either on the reformers or on the mat. Great for beginers to exercise in pairs!
